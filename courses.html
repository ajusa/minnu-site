Course Information

English espresso offers courses for all ages be it children, young learners, adults, Our team understands that each one of us is at a different level on their path to learning the English language. In these classes, you're not just learning English, you're learning how to take your English communication to the next level.

Our curriculum includes activities like Pecha-Kucha, show and tell, word race, Impromptu speech, phonetics, Personality development, Confidence build-up sessions, etc to learn everything from reading, writing, listening, and speaking. Also, to provide personal attention to Individuals, we only accommodate 6 learners in 1 Batch.

English One
Our flagship course comprises of 24 sessions of face to face live interactive sessions, along with 4 feedback sessions and 2 assessment tests at regular intervals. Students are also asked to undergo an additional 4 sessions of foundation course to ascertain everybody at the same learning level. At the end of the course, we expect the student to be able to communicate and express themselves confidently in everyday situations.
English Swift
Our personalized course comprises of 12 sessions of face to face interactive sessions, feedback session, and assessment test. The personalized course gives you the liberty to choose 6 sessions of your own interest from our comprehensive curriculum. This course is ideal for people who are short on time or would want to fast track their learning experience.
Student Special
Our early development course offers 12 monthly face to face interactive sessions along with feedback sessions and assessment tests at regular intervals. The course has been designed for all students keeping in mind their dynamic needs at different ages, delivering an experience like never before. We believe that it takes 6 months or more for a student to attain a level of Language confidence. Therefore, 6 months are mandatory for one to enroll for this course.
